**Last updated:** March 16, 2026

## Standing Design Principles

These were established in the March 10 working session with Carlos and are non-negotiable unless revisited with him.

1. **External focus.** The course is about building AI products that win — not internal team transformation. Internal topics are covered by AIPC and other programs.
2. **Senior + AI-aware audience.** Participants know AI fundamentals. No need to teach tokens, RAG, or prompting basics.
3. **Dejan leads content.** Same model as Vibe Coding. Daria supports and accelerates. GitHub repo is the single source of truth.
4. **Artifact-driven.** One living Strategy Brief built across 6 sessions — not worksheets stapled at the end.
5. **Opinionated tone.** The course has a point of view. Conventional wisdom is challenged in every module.
