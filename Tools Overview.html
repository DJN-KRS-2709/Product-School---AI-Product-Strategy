Persistent Tool
Living Strategy Dashboard
Interactive visual dashboard with vulnerability bars, flywheel radar, live cost curves with scenario sliders, trust meter, and maturity spider chart. Board View mode for presentations. Strategy health score. Imports from all module tools.

The Connected Loop

Each module's tool saves to your browser (localStorage) and feeds the Living Strategy Dashboard. Every tool has an "Open Living Strategy" link that reinforces this connection. The dashboard renders live visualizations from your data — vulnerability bars, flywheel radar, cost curves with what-if sliders, trust meters, and maturity spider charts. Toggle "Board View" for a clean, presentation-ready display. The Pitch Assembler and AI Bet Evaluator use your complete strategy for the final presentation and self-assessment.
