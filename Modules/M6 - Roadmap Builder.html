Roadmap Builder

Pull your strategy README, drop a Jira / spreadsheet export of your backlog, and generate the AI prompt that maps every initiative to a strategy component and a horizon — ready to paste into ChatGPT or Claude.

How It Works
1
Load your strategy README — the one the Strategy Assembler built. Repo URL, file upload, or paste — whichever works.
2
Drop your backlog — a Jira CSV export, a Linear/Asana/Notion export, or any spreadsheet of initiatives. The parser figures out the columns automatically.
3
Review & edit — confirm the parsed initiatives, remove noise, add anything Jira doesn't capture (stakeholder asks, half-formed ideas).
4
Generate the prompt — copy into Claude or ChatGPT. The output is a 3-horizon roadmap with each initiative mapped to a strategy component, plus an unmapped pile to cut. Paste the result into 06-the-pitch/roadmap.md.
