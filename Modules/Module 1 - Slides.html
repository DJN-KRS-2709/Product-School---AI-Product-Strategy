Provocation · 8 Minutes

True or False?

Vote first → reveal → debate.
FALSE
"The best AI product wins."
OpenAI, Google, Apple ship native features — great UX ≠ a moat. Ex: calendar AI vs standalone “AI scheduling” apps.
FALSE
"You can predict which AI feature wins pre-launch."
Outputs are distributions. Big wins often = new behaviors (Shopify, Gamma) — pattern-matching misses them.
FALSE
"Your discovery is fast enough for the AI era."
Classic cycles: 6–8 weeks. AI-assisted loops: days (Notion-class teams). Quarterly research = asymmetric risk.
