  • Present your top encroachment threat
The Encroacher
  • "I'm Head of Product at [Google/OpenAI/Microsoft]. Here's my 90-day plan."
  • Week 1–4: what you ship. Week 5–8: how you poach users. Week 9–12: why they don't come back
  • Target the weakest flywheel loop — that's your entry point

After both rounds: write down the single most dangerous move you heard.

Framework · 3 Minutes

The Kill Switch

Swap any AI provider in under 48 hours.
1
Abstraction Layer
No direct API calls to any provider in product code.
2
Multi-Model Routing
Tasks route by cost, latency, quality. Provider fails? Traffic shifts.
3
Eval Harness
Automated tests prove the new provider hits your quality bar. Switch with confidence.

Can't swap in 48 hours? That's not a strategy — it's a dependency.
