Lecture · Agent Governance

Agent Governance

Software picks actions—not just steps—lock these four.
Autonomy
Draft ≠ send · read ≠ write—explicit space
Tool Calls
Whitelist · rate-limit · log—each call = exfil risk
Memory
What persists · TTL · who reads—HIPAA/regs
Chain
B fails on A—named owner per handoff

Microsoft Copilot + Graph/plugins = production orchestration—Gokul: principles beat model names.

Key Takeaways

The Guardrails

Compounding beats scaling. Most AI products scale without getting smarter. If your system doesn't compound — corrections feeding training, cross-domain transfer, network intelligence — it's a commodity with variable costs.

Governance done well is a GTM asset. Most orgs plateau at compliance (Level 1). Enterprise buyers increasingly ask for governance artifacts. The jump from compliance to sellable trust accelerates deals.

Shadow AI is a strategy failure, not a compliance gap. Samsung's lesson: three data leaks in a month, no policy, no trail. The absence of governance isn't neutral — it's actively degrading your AI strategy.
