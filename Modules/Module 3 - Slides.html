Provocation

Before It Expands Them.

FALSE

Seats = access. AI often = work done — seat pricing under-captures value.
FALSE

Lecture · Bundling Strategy

Leaders, Fillers & Killers

Bundle wrong and you give the farm away.
Leader
What users come for — must-have, high WTP
AI: core intelligence (e.g. GitHub Copilot autocomplete)
Filler
Nice add for marginal price — bumps ARPU
AI: summaries, translations, templates
Killer
Kills the bundle if included — sell as add-on
AI: heavy inference (image gen, agent workflows)

70% rule: If >70% of users use the AI feature → bundle it. <70% with high value → add-on.

Gokul: Raise price on proven value. Don't only shave tokens — do both.

Key Takeaways

The Margin

AI products have fundamentally different economics. Traditional SaaS runs at 80%+ gross margins. AI introduces variable costs that scale with usage — power users can blow up your unit economics.

Model cascading is the highest-leverage margin lever. Routing 80% of requests to a cheap model and 20% to frontier can transform a -15% stress scenario into +30% margin.

How you charge matters more than how much. Most pricing failures happen because teams never figured out the pricing model, not the price point. Outcome-based pricing captures AI value; seat-based doesn't.
