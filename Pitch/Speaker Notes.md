## Slide 15 — Decisions Needed

- 4 decisions. Ask each one. Wait for response.
  1. **Module titles:** "The Bet / The Moat / The Margin..." — recommend strategic questions over evolution framing
  2. **M4/M5 overlap:** strategic 20%. Connect, don't duplicate.
  3. **Tone:** commit to True/False energy every module. Softening after M1 = bait-and-switch.
  4. **Own product vs. shared fictive company:** recommend own product. Hybrid now — live diagnosis on shared product, all applied work on theirs. Shared company = easier peer work but becomes a case study course. Own product = "board-ready Monday morning" promise stays real.
- If Carlos defers: "Can we align on #1 and #4 today? Those shape everything."
